Non-Chapter Books

When I was younger, I liked Frog and Toad, Garfield, The Lorax, Horton Hears a Who, and many others. My sister liked The Baby Animal Rescue Fairies. She is still obsessed with it. She made a whole WEBSITE about it! You should listen to these sorts of books, then start reading them yourself.(Ask an older sibling to read to you, it will probably be SUPER annoying for them)

Graphic Novels

Once you've started reading on your own, you should wait a little while and then try out graphic novels. Graphic novels are a little longer, but they still have pictures.(phew!) Sometimes they have chapters, but not all. I liked Diary Of a Wimpy Kid, Bone, Big Nate, Captain Underpants, Tintin, Asterix, and more.
